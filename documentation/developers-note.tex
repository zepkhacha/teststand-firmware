This user guide is for the Clock \& Controls Center (CCC) for the Muon \gm2 experiment at Fermi National Accelerator Laboratory.  The CCC was developed to distribute a high-precision clock and synchronization signals to the experiment's various subsystems.  For information on the experimental requirements and design choices, please refer to the appropriate sections of the Technical Design Report.
